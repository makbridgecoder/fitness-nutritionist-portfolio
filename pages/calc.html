A calories calculator helps estimate your daily caloric requirements based on key factors such as age, weight, height, gender, and activity level. TDEE stands for Total Daily Energy Expenditure. It represents the total number of calories your body burns in a day, including all activities. TDEE is calculated by combining your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) with the calories burned through physical activity, digestion, and daily tasks. It helps determine how many calories you need to maintain, lose, or gain weight. Factors that affect TDEE include age, gender, weight, height, and activity level. Knowing your TDEE is essential for creating an effective and personalized nutrition or fitness plan.
